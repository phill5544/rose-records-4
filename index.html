About Rebel Rose

Julia Rose Warren is a punk-rock painter slashing tradition with skulls, spray paint, and sound. She turns vinyl records into raw tributes to rebellion, rage, and rhythm. Rebel Rose Studios isn’t just an art brand — it's a movement for misfits and music freaks alike.
